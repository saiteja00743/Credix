## FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

**Q1: Is this actually free?**
Yes. The audit is completely free. No credit card, no trial period. We make money when you book a Credex consultation to implement the savings — and only on the savings we actually find.

**Q2: How accurate are the savings numbers?**
Every recommendation traces to an official vendor pricing page URL, verified weekly. We flag when pricing might have changed. The audit engine logic is publicly documented in our `PRICING_DATA.md`.

**Q3: Do you store my data?**
We store the audit result (not your actual invoices — just the numbers you type in) to generate your shareable report link. Your data is never sold or shared. See our privacy policy.

**Q4: What if my savings are low?**
We tell you honestly. If your stack is well-optimized, the report will say so, explain why, and suggest what to watch for next quarter as pricing evolves. We don't manufacture savings that don't exist.

**Q5: What's the next step after the audit?**
If your identified savings exceed $5,000/year, you'll see a CTA to book a free 30-minute Credex consultation. We walk through the recommendations together and, if you want, handle the vendor negotiations for you on a success-fee basis.
